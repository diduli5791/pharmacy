About Us


HEAVEN OF HEALTH, we are committed to offering quality health care services to our valued customers through the employment of internationally recognized treatment facilities, coupled with state-of-the-art medical equipment


Our service and laboratory facilities are internationally accredited, and we adhere to the tightest medical standards recognized by the international and the medical council of Sri Lanka.


Our cost-effective services and maintenance of high medical standards are a testimony to our commitment for caring services to our customers.
